PDXShelter

Bringing real time shelter information to those who need it most.

Research shows that 70% of homeless youth have access to smartphones.

We are a nonprofit organization dedicated to bringing critical resource information on demand.

Built for both web and mobile devices, we hope to provide a platform everyone can use, even the illiterate.



A full dashboard for shelters to expedite checkins, provide intake information, and update availabilities.

Streamlining the process of vetting guests, shelters are able to focus more of their time on their guest's needs.

Using geolocation, we are building a model that can be implemented in any city worldwide without changing a single line of code.
